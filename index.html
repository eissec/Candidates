About the Candidates

The FIDE Candidates Tournament is one of the most important FIDE events of the year. Players compete for the right to play in the next FIDE World Championship match against current chess champion GM Ding Liren.

Players compete for the right to challenge GM Ding Liren in the 2024 World Chess Championship and a share of the €500,000 prize fund.

Players compete in a double round-robin. The time control is 120 minutes for the first 40 moves, followed by 30 more minutes for the rest of the game. There's a 30-second increment starting on move 41.
